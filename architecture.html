Transaction Flow Process
1
Contract Creation
Parties create and digitally sign smart contract terms
2
Fiat Deposit
Client transfers funds to Esqro's regulated bank account
3
Token Issuance
Platform generates equivalent tokens and locks them in smart contract
4
Milestone Tracking
System monitors contract conditions and milestone completion
5
Automated Release
Smart contract releases tokens when conditions are met
6
Fiat Conversion
Tokens are converted to fiat and transferred to recipient's bank
7
Token Destruction
Used tokens are permanently destroyed from the system
